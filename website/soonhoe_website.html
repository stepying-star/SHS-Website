Soon Hoe Management Group is a leading professional consulting management company committed to providing comprehensive financial, tax, operational management and corporate secretarial services. Backed by an experienced team and extensive industry resources, we deliver efficient, tailored solutions that optimise business processes, improve management effectiveness and ensure compliant operations.

With Singapore as our financial hub, we specialise in offering bespoke overseas living and investment services to high-net-worth individuals and families across Southeast Asia and Greater China. Our services span overseas asset allocation, identity planning, children's education, family office management and premium concierge services — helping clients steadily achieve their wealth management and global lifestyle goals.
